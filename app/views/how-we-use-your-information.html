How we’ll use the information you provide

How we process your application 

We will check the information you have provided with the early years provider where you currently work.

How we pay you

DfE will make the payment directly to you.

To complete the rest of your application, you will need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank account details

Student loan deductions

If you’re currently paying off a student loan, a deduction from your payment will go towards repaying it.

Income Tax

We’ll pay your Income Tax and National Insurance contributions up to the basic tax rate of 20%, which is currently £12,571 to £50,270.

We can not pay any Income Tax and National Insurance contributions over the higher rate of 40%, which is currently £50,271 to £125,140.
