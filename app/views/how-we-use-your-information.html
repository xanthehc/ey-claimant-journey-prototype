How we’ll use the information you provide

How we process your claim 

We'll check the information you've given us with the early years provider where you currently work.

How we pay you

The Department for Education will make the payment directly to you.

Student loan deductions

If you’re currently paying off a student loan, a deduction from your payment will go towards repaying it.

Income Tax

We’ll pay your Income Tax and National Insurance contributions up to the basic tax rate of 20%, which is currently £12,571 to £50,270.

We cannot pay any Income Tax and National Insurance contributions over the higher rate of 40%, which is currently £50,271 to £125,140.
